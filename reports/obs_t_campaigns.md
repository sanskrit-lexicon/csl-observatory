> **Why this matters (H3).** The diachronic component trends are not purely organic: a sizeable share of early correction volume comes from **discrete bulk campaigns** — one-off IAST/meta-line conversions and AS-number passes — that ran and finished. A "falling encoding/orthography" trend is partly the signature of those conversions completing, not only gradual drift. This separates campaign-driven from organic change.
