## Reading

- Half of all issues that get closed are closed within **7 days** — but the distribution has a very long tail (p90 = 472 days): what is not handled quickly tends to sit for years.
- **23% of issues are still open one year after opening** (pooled cohorts, censoring-adjusted); the survival table shows whether recent cohorts fare better or worse than the 2014–2018 era.
- The backlog is old: 79% of currently-open issues are more than two years old, and **157 open issues have never received a single comment** — the silent backlog is the natural first target for triage (close, label, or answer).
- Per-repo medians differ by an order of magnitude; the slowest large repos are where a scholar-contributor's issue is most likely to die unanswered.
