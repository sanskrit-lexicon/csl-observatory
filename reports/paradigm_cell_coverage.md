**Ceiling.** DCS tags unaccented text — it cannot split verb class I/VI or IV/passive at the root-class level, and `Tense=Past` conflates aorist/perfect. A "cell" here is a DCS UD-tag 5-tuple (tense, mood, voice, person, number) on **finite** tokens only (`feat_verbform IS NULL`); non-finite forms (participle, converb, gerundive, infinitive) are excluded — they are nominal derivatives with a different paradigm shape. Two traditionally-distinct lakara cells that collapse to the same UD tag will undercount here; this method never overcounts.
