## 0 · Verdict — where the sanskrit-util payoff is (and isn't)

The ×62/×170 headline numbers **did not shrink and were never going to**: `transcoder.py`/`digentry.py` copies are generation-time vendored instances of the dict-build toolchain — sanskrit-util explicitly left that lane alone ([SHARED_CODE.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/github-spine/blob/main/SHARED_CODE.md) §1–2: "transcoder.py for build pipelines, sanskrit-util for keys and app transcode"). The rest of the queue is Wave-2 work, most of it explicitly deferred/blocked in SHARED_CODE.md.
