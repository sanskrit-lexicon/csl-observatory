# Benchmarks

How CSL compares to peer digital lexicography / corpus projects.

## Comparison matrix (planned)

| Project | Domain | Started | Open source | Issues tracked publicly | Repos | API |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CDSL** (this) | Sanskrit lexicography | 2014 | Yes (CC-BY-SA + GPL) | Yes (GitHub) | 77 | csl-apidev |
| Thesaurus Linguae Graecae (TLG) | Greek lexicography | 1972 | No | No | n/a | Subscription |
| Perseus Digital Library | Greek + Latin lexicon + corpus | 1985 | Yes | Partial (issues on GitHub) | ~30 | Yes |
| Cuneiform Digital Library Initiative (CDLI) | Sumerian / Akkadian | 2000 | Yes | Yes (GitHub) | ~10 | Yes |
| DDBDP (Duke Databank Documentary Papyri) | Papyrology | 1982 | Yes | Yes (papyri.info) | ~5 | Yes |
| Pandanus (Czech Sanskrit dict) | Sanskrit lexicography | 2002 | Yes | No | ~3 | Limited |
| Sanskrit Heritage (G. Huet) | Sanskrit morphology | 1995 | Yes (LGPL) | Personal site | ~8 | Yes |
| Digital Corpus of Sanskrit (DCS) | Sanskrit corpus + lexicon | 2007 | Yes | Yes | ~5 | Yes |

*Numbers in cells like "~30" are estimates pending validation. We have working contacts at Perseus and Sanskrit Heritage to verify.*
