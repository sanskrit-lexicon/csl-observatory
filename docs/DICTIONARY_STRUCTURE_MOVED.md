# Dictionary Structure Work Moved

Date: 2026-06-03

Dictionary-structure research no longer lives in `csl-observatory`.

Moved to `csl-atlas`:

- lexicographic genealogy and sanhw1 inheritance outputs;
- Patel headword-convention and L0 cladogram work;
- dictionary microstructure / macrostructure planning;
- R2 sense-alignment and sense-granularity work;
- forensic dictionary-lineage scripts, data, and Article 21.
- etymology-style witness page (H1525): frozen probes
  `data/etymology_marker_preliminary.csv` and `data/wil_nirukta_tokens.csv`
  are rendered at
  [csl-atlas `/tools/etymology-style`](https://sanskrit-lexicon.github.io/csl-atlas/tools/etymology-style)
  (atlas PR [#293](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-atlas/pull/293)).

`csl-observatory` remains the GitHub/org observatory: repositories, issues,
pull requests, contributors, workflows, runbooks, and digitisation-work metrics.
